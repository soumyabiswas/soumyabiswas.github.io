E-Learning Forum

Make Learning easy, interactive and fun

E-Learning Forum is a web platform that allows users to collaborate with the tuitors for effective learning, where users can post their doubts and get it solved by the experts.

With the outbreak of Corona, the traditional classroom based learning has become almost impossible, which laid to the basis of this online learning forum

Key Features
  • Post A Question and get it answered by experts
  • Post Assignments For students.
  • Post Reference Material like pdfs, videos, images.
  • Quiz for students assignment

In summary, what we built is: (YE TUM BATA DO :p)

  • international marketplace (built in Ruby on Rails) allowing end-customers to browse tourist tours and attractions, see their schedule and prices, and book them online;
  • huge single page application for Partners (built in React and Redux) to manage their offers, schedules, prices, orders, customers and agents’ network;
  • custom widgets written in JS, which Partners can put in order to advertise and sell their tours directly on their website;
  • an ability for marketplace to list and sell the products under a Partner’s or an Agent’s own branding and logo (and his own commision);
  • notifications and orders management centre for customers and tour organisers/pilots.
Technologies used
  • React
  • Bootstrap
  • Spring Boot
  • PostgreSQL
